# reMELD-Na — Google Play Store Descriptions

---

## 🇬🇧 English

**App name:** reMELD-Na

**Short description (80 chars max):**
Calculate and track reMELD-Na scores for liver transplant candidates.

**Full description:**
reMELD-Na is a medical calculator for the Refitted Model for End-Stage Liver Disease with Sodium (reMELD-Na) score — the scoring system used by EuroTransplant to assess severity of chronic liver disease and predict 90-day mortality in patients awaiting liver transplantation.

**Features:**
• Calculate reMELD-Na score from blood test values
• Supports mg/dL and µmol/L units for creatinine and bilirubin
• Automatic value adjustments for dialysis patients
• Automatic INR adjustment for patients taking blood-thinning tablets
• All EuroTransplant limits applied automatically
• Save scores with blood collection date
• Track score history over time
• Export data as CSV or JSON
• Share data via email, WhatsApp, or cloud storage
• Available in 8 languages: English, German, Portuguese, Dutch, French, Croatian, Hungarian, Slovenian
• All data stored locally on your device — no internet required
• Free and open source (MIT License)

**Medical disclaimer:** This app is a clinical decision support tool only. It does not replace clinical judgment.
